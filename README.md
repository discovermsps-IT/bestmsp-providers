## What Are Managed Service Providers?
Managed Service Providers (MSPs) are companies that manage your IT systems 
and infrastructure so you don’t need an in-house IT team.
  Businesses rely on MSPs for:
- Day-to-day IT support  
- Cloud setup and management  
- Cybersecurity protection  
- Network monitoring and maintenance  

In short,MSPs act as your extended IT team without high operational costs.
